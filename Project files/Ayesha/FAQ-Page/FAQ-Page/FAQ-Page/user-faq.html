Frequently Asked Questions

1) How to join the course? ➤
You can join the course by following the procedures provided by the institute. The admin updates the instructions regularly.
2) Why to join the institute? ➤
The institute offers expert faculty, structured courses, lab facilities, and career guidance for students.
3) When will Entrance Examinations be conducted? ➤
Entrance exams are held every 6 months. Students should check the website regularly for dates and fees.
4) Can I use the Lab facilities after my class hours? Any hidden charges? ➤
Yes, labs remain open till 9 PM during course days with no extra charges. After completion, lab usage is paid ($1000 if pre-opted, $1500 if later).
5) How can I apply for the entrance exam? ➤
Applications are accepted online or via paper forms at the centre. Students must choose their desired course while applying.
6) Will there be any fees for the entrance exam? ➤
Yes, entrance exam fees will be mentioned clearly on the application form.
7) How to make the payment? ➤
Payments can be made via cash, draft, or cheque at the institute. Online details like cheque/DD number and bank info must be entered. After acceptance, acknowledgement is mailed with exam details.
