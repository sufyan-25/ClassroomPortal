Who We Are

Toyo Toys was founded in 2024 with a simple goal — to create toys that inspire imagination and bring endless fun to children around the world. Our team of dedicated designers, engineers, and dreamers combine creativity with innovation to deliver quality toys that spark joy and learning.

We believe every toy tells a story — one that starts with laughter, exploration, and creativity. From concept to final product, our focus remains on safety, durability, and pure fun.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make learning and play go hand in hand. We design toys that encourage creativity, critical thinking, and joy — giving children the tools to explore their imagination freely and safely.

Every product we make carries our promise of quality, innovation, and happiness — because for us, a toy is more than an object, it’s a gateway to a child’s world of wonder.

Toyo Toys Overview

Toyo Toys is a modern toy company offering a wide range of creative and educational products. We focus on safety, innovation, and fun for all ages.
